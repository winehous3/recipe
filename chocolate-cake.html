Chocolate cake

How to bake the winehouse's chocolate cake

Informations

  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Additional Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hr
  • Servings: 24
  • Yield: 2 to 9 - inch round cake layers

Mix Dry Ingredients

Stir or whisk together suger, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until evenly combined.

Add Wet Ingredients

add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, Mix on medium speed for about two minutes. Stir in boiling water. Don't worry if the batter seems a little thin, as reviewers say that's perfectly normal.

Bake

Pour the cake batter into prepared pans. Bake for about half an hour, until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted in the middle. Let cool and top with your favorite frosting.

How to Store Chocolate Cake

The best way to store this one-bowl chocolate cake depends on the frosting you choose. A buttercream-frosted cake can Sti (covered) at room temperature for up to five days. If yo use cream cheese frosting, though, you'll want to refrigerate it within two hours.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.

  3. Step 3

    In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.

  4. Step 4

    Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.

  5. Step 5

    Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin.

  6. Step 6

    Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

  7. Step 7

    Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.

  8. Step 8

    Enjoy!
